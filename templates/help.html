Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

Here are some commonly asked questions to assist you in using the system:

Q1: What if I encounter issues during facial authentication?

A: Ensure good lighting and maintain the recommended distance from the camera. Follow the on-screen instructions 
for capturing your face accurately. If issues persist, contact our support team.

Q2: Can I change my vote after submitting it?

A: No, once a vote is submitted, it cannot be changed. Please make your selections carefully.

Q3: How can I reset my password?

A: Contact our support team to reset your password.

Q4: Is my vote confidential?

A: Yes, your vote is confidential. The system ensures anonymity, and your vote cannot be linked to your identity.

Q5: Can I vote multiple times?

A: No, the system allows only one vote per eligible user. Attempting to vote multiple times is not permitted.

Q6: What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

A: If you experience technical difficulties, contact our support team for assistance. Provide details about the issue you're facing for faster resolution.

Q7: How is my data protected?

A: We take data security seriously. Your personal information and voting data are encrypted and stored securely. 
We follow industry best practices to protect your data.
